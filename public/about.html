About AstroBridge

Building elegant solutions for WhatsApp automation

Our Mission

AstroBridge was created to provide developers with a powerful, yet simple middleware layer for WhatsApp automation. We believe in building tools that are elegant, reliable, and accessible to everyone.

Our goal is to make WhatsApp integration seamless, allowing developers to focus on building amazing applications without worrying about the complexities of the underlying protocol.

Core Values

  • Simplicity — Easy to use, easy to understand
  • Reliability — Stable connections you can count on
  • Performance — Optimized for speed and efficiency
  • Open Source — Community-driven development
